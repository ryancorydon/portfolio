About Me

Hello, I'm Ryan Corydon

I'm a Business Intelligence Analyst based in Chicago. I have a Management Information Systems degree from Iowa State University with a minor in Data Science, and 2+ years of experience designing enterprise-grade dashboards and modernizing analytics platforms. My work centers on Power BI, Snowflake, SQL, and Tableau, and I specialize in translating complex data into clear, actionable insight for leadership teams.

At The Waldinger Corporation, I lead BI platform modernization efforts — administering GitHub workflows for analytics engineering, deploying executive-level Power BI dashboards, and mentoring team members along the way. I got my start building interactive Tableau dashboards for Iowa State University's Extension Group, and I've been hooked on turning data into decisions ever since.

Outside of work, I enjoy rooting on all Chicago sports teams, especially the Bears and the Cubs. I also enjoy watching professional soccer while supporting Chelsea. Looking ahead, my goal is to improve my golf game and travel more.
